Welcome, wanderer, welcome!

⚪️ As a child, I was forced urged to learn the piano. I am grateful now but as a kid, I did not appreciate the early Saturday morning lessons. My brothers and I were trained by the same piano teacher, Mr. Leroy Moore. I owe my strong attention to detail, dexterity, and second-hand pointers in extreme patience to him!
⚪️ In the midst of piano, books and daydreaming, I developed a strong desire to learn about structures and art. After high school, I moved to San Antonio to attend college and studied both Architecture and Interior Environmental Design from the University of Texas at San Antonio and the University of the Incarnate Word, respectively. The two subjects gave me the knowledge & foundation to go forth and begin my career as a designer.
⚪️ After graduation, I had the pleasure of working with the City Architect's office in San Antonio and got to work on some pretty cool projects. My favorite projects were the Tobin Center for the Performing Arts and the many renovation projects along the Riverwalk (which we got to travel to by the city's own river barge!). I sometimes could not believe what I was involved with at such a young age and valued every moment I had there.
⚪️ After the City, I worked with a San Antonio-based architecture firm which handled government projects. We completed renovations and new construction for military bases, air fields, schools, and various ports of entry along the Texas/Mexico border.
⚪️ A few years later, I relocated to Dallas to begin a new adventure as a 3D Branded Environment designer for a production company which produced live corporate events around the world. It was by far, the coolest job I have ever had. Our clients were many, but most notably we worked with companies such as: Tableau, Mary Kay, Cetera Financial Group, Apple, HBO, and many more. These live events were epic, for the lack of a better word. Each year, our clients desired bigger and better shows, sometimes feeling more like a rock concert/carnival masquerading as a corporate event - which was okay with me! We designed stages speakers, one-of-a-kind bar & coffee shops, and various "instagrammable moments", providing them with a memorable experience. It was incredibly amazing, at times extremely challenging, but all in all, the best experience I've had to date.
⚪️ All these paths have led me to my new career as a full stack web developer. What a long, strange trip it's been... I look forward to partnering with you and creating something fantastic.
