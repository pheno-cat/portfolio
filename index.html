⚪️ As a child, I was forced urged to learn the piano. I am grateful now but as a kid, I did not appreciate the early Saturday morning lessons. My brothers and I were trained by the same piano teacher, Mr. Leroy Moore. I owe my strong attention to detail, dexterity, and second-hand pointers in extreme patience to him!
⚪️ In the midst of piano, books and daydreaming, I developed a strong desire to learn about structures and art. After high school, I moved to San Antonio to attend college and studied both Architecture and Interior Environmental Design from the University of Texas at San Antonio and the University of the Incarnate Word, respectively. The two subjects gave me the knowledge & foundation to go forth and begin my career as a designer.
⚪️ After graduation, I had the pleasure of working with the City Architect's office in San Antonio and got to work on some pretty cool projects. My favorite projects were the Tobin Center for the Performing Arts and the many renovation projects along the Riverwalk. I sometimes could not believe what I was involved with at such a young age and valued every moment I had there.
⚪️ After the City, I worked with a San Antonio-based architecture firm which handled government projects. We completed renovations and new construction for military bases, air fields, schools, and various ports of entry along the Texas/Mexico border.
⚪️ A few years later, I relocated to Dallas to begin a new adventure as a 3D Branded Environment designer for a production company which produced live corporate events around the world. It was by far the coolest job I have ever had. Our clients were many, but most notably we worked with companies such as: Tableau, Mary Kay, Northwestern Mutual, Apple, HBO, Disney and many more. These live events were epic, for the lack of a better word. Each year, our clients desired bigger and better shows which challenged me as a designer to stay current with new technologies and design trends. We designed stages for speakers & presentations, one-of-a-kind, branded bar & coffee shops, and various "instagrammable moments", providing them with a memorable experience. It was incredibly amazing, at times extremely challenging, but all in all, the best experience I've had to date.
⚪️ I have learned that no role is too small or insignificant. Teams thrive when there is mutual understanding, communication, encouragement, and respect. I look forward to applying my past experiences and newfound knowledge to my next projects and teams.
